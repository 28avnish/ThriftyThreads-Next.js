PONJOUNG

Ponjoung is a contemporary sans serif font that boasts a modern yet playful feel. Simple geometric shapes combined with unique accents make this font very fresh and special yet suitable for any purposes.

Ponjoung comes in 4 weights, plus matching italics also includes swashes on capital letters and stylistic alternates, which can be easily accessed from the OpenType menu.

This font is perfect for corporate needs, promotional designs, brand identity, websites and more.
